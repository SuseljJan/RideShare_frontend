What is Shryder

Sick of public transportation? Want to get to your destination faster and cheaper and meet new people in between? Want to travel on longer distances Shryder is an application which helps You share transportations. It is meant for those which are looking for a ride as well as for those who wish to share a ride with a stranger in exchange for a price which will cover part of the cost of the transportation

What are campaigns?

Each driver gets many transport requests on each of their transport offers. Which is why we want to make it available to you, as a passenger to be able to send multiple transport requests to different transport offers even when you'll you're only planning to attend one of them.

When submitting a transport request you'll be prompted to select a campaign under which you wish to submit the transport request. You can submit many transport requests to different transport offers under the same campaign. When you get selected to one of the transport offers, the campaign under which you sent transport request will be closed, meaning all other transport requests you sent under the same campaign will be deactivated. screenshot of some sort of graph... screenshot of creating campaign... screenshot of creating transport request where user picks campaign...
